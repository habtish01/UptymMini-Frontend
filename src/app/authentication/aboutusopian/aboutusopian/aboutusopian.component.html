ABOUT US

At Eshe, we believe that everyone deserves access to quality healthcare. That's why we've created a virtual clinic platform that connects patients with virtual clinics for healthcare services and treatment. Our aim is to revolutionize the healthcare industry in Ethiopia and beyond by leveraging technology to improve access to care and treatment.

Eshe addresses these challenges head-on by providing a mobile platform that connects patients with virtual clinics for healthcare services and treatment. Our platform includes virtual visits and consultations, a service directory of medical institutions and services, and a patient portal where patients can access and manage their healthcare data. We also facilitate online consultations between doctors, reducing misdiagnoses and improving the quality of care at peripheral health facilities.

Features

VIRTUAL CONSULTAIONS

Eshe enables patients to access virtual visits and consultations with their healthcare providers through video conferencing, chat, or phone.

SERVICE DIRECTORY

Eshe provides a service directory listing of medical institutions and services ready and available to provide care. We make it easier for patients to locate and evaluate health institutions and their quality.

PATIENT PORTAL

Eshe gives patients access to a patient portal where they can view/see their healthcare data decoded to provide them an understanding of their health status.

DOCTOR TO DOCTOR CONSULTAIONS

Doctors at peripheral health facilities can have online consultations with specialists at higher level institutions through Eshe platform. Eshe supports medical decision making through facilitation of consultations with seniors.
